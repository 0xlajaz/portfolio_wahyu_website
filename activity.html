🎨 Head of Creative Media Department

As the Head of Creative Media at the Data Science Student Association (HMSD), ITERA, I led three main divisions: Media & Content Specialist, Publication & Documentation, and Visual Design. Our team of 19 members worked together to enhance the organization's branding through engaging content on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

Throughout 2024, we successfully developed numerous creative campaigns, including promotional videos, event documentation, and interactive educational content to improve community engagement and organizational visibility.

Duration: January - November 2024

🏆 1st Place in Videography & Best Presentation

This event was organized by the Pertamina Workers Union, Marketing Unit III in Bandung on November 1-3, 2024. It focused on discussing Indonesia's national energy sovereignty issues.

During this competition, I won both 1st place in videography and Best Presentation, focusing on how renewable energy can enhance Indonesia's energy independence.

To strengthen my arguments in both the video production and presentation, I ensured that my topic was rich in data analysis. I utilized quantitative insights, statistical models, and visual analytics to provide a solid foundation for every claim. This helped in demonstrating the potential and feasibility of renewable energy in Indonesia, making my arguments more compelling and evidence-driven.

Date: November 1-3, 2024

⛽ Pertamina Study Visit - FT Bandung Group Padalarang

On November 7, 2024, we had the opportunity to visit Pertamina at FT Bandung Group Padalarang. During this study visit, we learned about the distribution process of Pertamina products from upstream to downstream.

The visit provided insights into how energy products are managed efficiently and safely, ensuring their availability across Indonesia.

🎤 Seminar Nasional Energi - Pertamina

This presentation was part of the National Energy Seminar Competition organized by Pertamina. I represented Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA) in this event, along with other finalists from various universities across Indonesia.

The topic I presented was "Langkah Berani Menuju Energi Mandiri dan Berkelanjutan". The presentation focused on how phasing out fuel subsidies can be a great opportunity to boost renewable energy development and ensure long-term economic resilience.

Key points discussed:
✔ Reducing subsidies to encourage investment in renewable energy.
✔ Strengthening economic sustainability through energy independence.
✔ Policies and strategies to support a green transition.

🏛️ Senat KM-ITERA - Divisi Eksternal x Medinfo

Senat KM-ITERA (Senat Keluarga Mahasiswa ITERA) is the student senate responsible for determining major policies in student life and accommodating student aspirations at Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA).

The senate functions as a non-structural student organization under the guidance of the Vice Rector for Student Affairs.

I am part of the Divisi Eksternal x Medinfo, where we engage in:

✔ Disseminating important information related to student policies.

Being involved in this role has given me valuable experience in leadership, public relations, and media management while contributing to student advocacy and organizational transparency.
